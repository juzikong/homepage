Hello I'm

Yan Zhiqiang

I am a third-year PhD student studying astrophysics, currently living in Prague, Czech Republic. I am genuinely interested in how the world works. That is "Cosmology" in the language of an astrophysicist. However, I am taking a less direct but more reliable way to approach my interest. I focus my study on galaxies, which is a much smaller scale than the study of the entire Universe with much more abundant observations available. There are still many questions unanswered by human beings about galaxies -- how do the galaxies form and how do they evolve. We cannot truly believe that we understand the universe without understanding the observable building blocks of it.
